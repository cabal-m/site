"Cabal Online" is a free-to-play 3D fantasy massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by ESTsoft. The game was first released in South Korea in 2005 and has since gained popularity in various regions around the world.

"Cabal Online" is set in the fictional world of Nevareth, a land that was once prosperous but has been devastated by the forces of evil. Players embark on a journey to combat the sinister Cabal, a group of beings seeking to destroy the world.

The game features various character classes, each with unique abilities and playstyles. Examples of classes include Warrior, Wizard, Force Blader, and Force Archer. Players can choose a class that suits their preferred combat style and role in the game.
